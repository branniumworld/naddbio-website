Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

NADDBio ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website at www.naddbio.com.

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Submit our contact form (name, email, company, message)

We automatically collect certain technical information when you visit our site:

We use the information we collect to:

Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies for essential functionality. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. The cookies we use are:

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

These services may collect limited technical data as described in their respective privacy policies.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. Our website uses HTTPS encryption for all data transmission.

Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions and correspondence for as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and maintain our business records, typically no longer than 3 years.

About Kingsley

Senior physician leadership without permanent executive overhead

Kingsley is a Senior Pharmaceutical Physician with 17+ years of global biopharma experience across clinical development (Phases I-IV), pharmacovigilance/drug safety, medical affairs, and regulatory strategy.

He is UK PMST-certified in Pharmaceutical Medicine and a Fellow of the Faculty of Pharmaceutical Medicine, UK Royal College of Physicians (FFPM-RCP).

Kingsley is a trusted advisor to biotech CEOs, CROs, and investors, providing fractional and consulting leadership from first-in-human through post-marketing.

He has proven ability to de-risk assets, guide regulator-ready programs, and support clinical & safety due diligence for licensing deals, M&A, and asset transactions.

Kingsley brings deep therapeutic breadth spanning cardiorenal-metabolic, oncology, inflammation/immunology, neuroscience, rare diseases, and vaccines.

As founder of NADDBio, he delivers senior physician leadership without permanent executive overhead - enabling biotech and pharma sponsors to access world-class expertise on demand.
